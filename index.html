A DEBATE ABOUT THE MEDITERRANEAN CRISIS BASED ON AEGEAN

About Greece, about Europe is a debate promoted by Waves of Youth, a Portuguese artistic collective, and by MEERU | Abrir Caminho, a non-governmental humanitarian organization, and supported by HUBB – Humans Before Borders and United Society of Balkans. About Greece, about Europe centres on Aegean, a documentary by Tomás Barão da Cunha and Raul Fretes, filmed during 2018 between Athens and Thessaloniki, south and northern Greece, respectively.
